Biography

I am a second-year PhD student in School of Computing at National University of Singapore, supervised by the Professor Roger Zimmermann and Provost’s Chair Professor David S. Rosenblum. I have published over ten papers in the refereed journals and conferences, such as TKDE, KDD, IJCAI, AAAI and UbiComp. Thanks to the supervision of Prof. Yu Zheng and Dr. Junbo Zhang, I started my research career at MSRA and graduated from Xidian University.

My research interests lie in deep learning, machine learning, and their applications in urban areas. If you are interested in my research, please feel free to contact me!
