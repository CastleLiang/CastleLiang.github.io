Yuxuan Liang(梁宇轩) is now in a joint Msc program between Xidian University (XDU) and Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA). Currently, he is a research intern on urban computing in Microsoft Research Asia, supervised by lead researcher Yu Zheng. His research interests include big data analytics, spatio-temporal data mining and ubiquitous computing. In Urban Computing Group, we are passionate about using big data to tackle urban challenges.
